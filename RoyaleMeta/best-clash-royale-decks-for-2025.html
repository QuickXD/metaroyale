Electro Giant Cycle

Win Rate: 68%
Usage: 12.4%
Difficulty: Medium
Average Elixir Cost: 3.6

Overall Strategy

This deck focuses on cycling back to Electro Giant as quickly as possible. The low-cost cycle cards (Skeletons, Ice Spirit, Bomber) help you defend efficiently and cycle back to your win condition. The key to success with this deck is managing your elixir effectively and knowing when to commit to a push.

In single elixir, focus on defense and making positive elixir trades. Only deploy Electro Giant when you have a significant elixir advantage or when you've successfully defended a push and have troops for a counter-push. In double elixir, you can be more aggressive with your Electro Giant placements.

Key Interactions

  • Electro Giant + Tornado: Pull enemy troops into the Electro Giant's zap radius to neutralize counters.
  • Lightning: Save for high-value targets like Inferno Tower, Inferno Dragon, or P.E.K.K.A.
  • Cannon + Ice Spirit: Effective against Hog Rider and other building-targeting units.
  • Bomber + Skeletons: Great defense against ground swarms and mini-tanks.

Card Substitutions

Firecracker provides more range and can hit air units, but is more vulnerable to spells.

Tesla costs more but can target air units and hide underground when not active.

Royal Champion Bait

Win Rate: 72%
Usage: 15.8%
Difficulty: Hard
Average Elixir Cost: 3.4

Overall Strategy

This deck uses multiple bait elements to force out specific counters, then capitalizes with either Royal Champion or Goblin Drill. The key to success is tracking your opponent's cycle and knowing when they don't have the right counters in hand.

In single elixir, focus on identifying your opponent's counters to your win conditions. Use Skeleton Army and other bait cards to force out spells, then punish with Goblin Drill. Save the Royal Champion for defense and counter-pushing. In double elixir, you can be more aggressive with your Royal Champion, using its ability to clear defensive units.

Key Interactions

  • Skeleton Army: Use to bait out small spells before deploying Goblin Drill.
  • Royal Champion + Ice Spirit: The Ice Spirit freezes units, allowing the Champion to eliminate them quickly.
  • Valkyrie + Goblin Drill: Place Valkyrie in front of counter-pushing troops, then Goblin Drill at the tower for a dual-lane attack.
  • Cannon + Ice Spirit: Effective against Hog Rider and other building-targeting units.

Card Substitutions

Goblin Gang is less vulnerable to small spells and can hit air units.

Poison provides area denial and is more effective against spawners and buildings.

Hog Earthquake

Win Rate: 65%
Usage: 10.2%
Difficulty: Medium
Average Elixir Cost: 3.3

Overall Strategy

Hog Earthquake is a fast-paced cycle deck that focuses on consistently getting Hog Rider to the tower with Earthquake support. The low average elixir cost allows you to cycle quickly and apply constant pressure throughout the match.

In single elixir, focus on making positive elixir trades and getting chip damage with Hog Rider. In double elixir, you can be more aggressive with your Hog Rider + Earthquake pushes, especially against building-heavy opponents.

Key Interactions

  • Hog Rider + Earthquake: Perfect for breaking through building defenses and dealing chip damage.
  • Tesla + Ice Spirit: Strong defensive combo against air and ground units.
  • Valkyrie + Firecracker: Excellent defense against swarm pushes.
  • Skeletons + Ice Spirit: Cheap cycle cards that provide surprising defensive value.

Card Substitutions

Musketeer provides more reliable damage but less splash potential.

Knight costs less elixir but has less splash damage capability.

Lavaloon

Win Rate: 62%
Usage: 8.5%
Difficulty: Hard
Average Elixir Cost: 4.1

Overall Strategy

Lavaloon is a powerful beatdown deck that focuses on building a massive air push with Lava Hound as the tank and Balloon as the primary damage dealer. The key to success is knowing when to commit to a push and how to defend efficiently with limited ground units.

In single elixir, focus on defense and making positive elixir trades. Only start a Lava Hound push if you have a significant elixir advantage. In double elixir, you can be more aggressive with your Lavaloon pushes, especially against opponents with weak air defenses.

Key Interactions

  • Lava Hound + Balloon: Core push combo, with Lava Hound tanking for the Balloon.
  • Skeleton Dragons + Mega Minion: Support air units that help clear defenses.
  • Tombstone: Excellent defensive building against ground units like Hog Rider and P.E.K.K.A.
  • Fireball + Zap: Clear air defenses like Musketeer, Wizard, or Inferno Tower.

Card Substitutions

Minions cost less elixir but are more vulnerable to splash damage.

Goblin Cage provides more offensive potential after defending.

Bridge Spam

Win Rate: 60%
Usage: 9.7%
Difficulty: Medium
Average Elixir Cost: 3.9

Overall Strategy

Bridge Spam is an aggressive deck that focuses on punishing opponents when they're low on elixir by "spamming" fast units at the bridge. P.E.K.K.A serves as your defensive powerhouse and counter-push tank, while Battle Ram, Bandit, and Royal Ghost are your primary offensive threats.

In single elixir, use P.E.K.K.A defensively and look for opportunities to counter-push. In double elixir, you can be more aggressive with your bridge spam tactics, especially when you know your opponent is low on elixir or has just used their counters.

Key Interactions

  • P.E.K.K.A: Excellent counter to tanks like Electro Giant, Golem, and Royal Giant.
  • Battle Ram + Bandit: Powerful dual-lane pressure combo.
  • Electro Wizard: Reset Inferno Tower/Dragon and counter Balloon and Sparky.
  • Poison + Zap: Clear swarms and support units around enemy towers.

Card Substitutions

Dark Prince offers more splash damage and shield protection.

Fireball provides instant damage instead of damage over time.
