Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2025

At RoyaleMeta.fun, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

1. What We Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not require users to create an account or submit personal data. If you contact us via email, we may receive your name and email address.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to understand how visitors use our site and to improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
  • Analytics: We use third-party tools (like Google Analytics) to collect anonymous traffic data (e.g. pages visited, browser type, device type).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your messages or feedback
  • To analyze site performance and improve features
  • To ensure website security and prevent abuse

3. Third-Party Services

We may use tools such as Google Analytics or advertising networks that might place their own cookies. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access to any personal data we might have (although likely none)
  • Request deletion of your email or any information you provided
  • Disable cookies in your browser

5. Children's Privacy

This site is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under the age of 13. If you're under 13, please do not submit any personal information.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to email us at: info@royalemeta.fun
