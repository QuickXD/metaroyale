A Tale of Thieves Season!

April 1, 2025 By RoyaleMeta Team

The April 2025 season brings a heist-themed adventure to Clash Royale with the new Champion Boss Bandit, Executioner Evolution, and a series of exciting events. Here's everything you need to know about "A Tale of Thieves" season!

New Champion: Boss Bandit

Ability: Getaway Grenade

Briefly becomes invisible and teleports backwards!

Boss Bandit dashes to her targets and is invulnerable while dashing. When things get tough, her smoke grenades are at the ready.

New Evolution: Executioner

That's not an axe... This is an axe!

Cycles: 1

Evolved Executioner's axe deals more damage to enemies in close quarters and pushes them back.

Season Events

Crown Chase: Wanted

March 31 - April 6

Earn Crowns to move forward in the event!

  • 1-Star Lucky Drop x2
  • 2-Star Lucky Drop
  • 3-Star Lucky Drop
  • Epic Book of Cards

Boss Bandit Event

April 10 - 15

Collect Crowns to unlock the new Champion, Boss Bandit, and other rewards!

  • 1-Star Lucky Drop x2
  • 2-Star Lucky Drop
  • Common Wild Card x100
  • Emote
  • Boss Bandit Card x2

Crown Chase

April 15 - 20

  • 1-Star Lucky Drop x4
  • 2-Star Lucky Drop
  • Common Wild Card x100
  • Rare Wild Card x50
  • Giant Snowball Evolution Shard x3
  • Banner Frame and Decoration

Community Event - Steal Like A Boss

Starts on April 21st

Show you're part of the gang by stealing Crowns from the Red King! The more Crowns you earn in Battles, the more rewards the community gets!

  • 1-Star Lucky Drop x5
  • 2-Star Lucky Drop
  • 3-Star Lucky Drop
  • Common Wild Card x100
  • Rare Wild Card x50
  • Legendary Wild Card
  • Banner Frame and Decoration
  • Tower Skin

Crown Chase

May 1 - 4

  • 1-Star Lucky Drop x5
  • 2-Star Lucky Drop x2
  • Common Wild Card x100
  • Epic Wild Card x10

Note: The Community Event and Crown Chase Events are available to all players starting from Arena 7. The Boss Bandit Event is available from Arena 11.

Check your event rewards progress right from the Home screen once the event is live.

Pro Tips for the New Season

  • Boss Bandit works exceptionally well with fast cycle decks - pair her with cheap cards to maximize her dash ability
  • Executioner Evolution counters swarm strategies effectively - use it against Goblin-heavy decks
  • Focus on the Community Event for the best rewards - coordinate with your clan to maximize Crown earnings
  • Save your Evolution Shards for Executioner if you play control decks
